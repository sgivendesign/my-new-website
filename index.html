Some cat information

It can take up to 2 years for kittens to reach their full size

On average, cats sleep between 12 and 16 hours per day

Cats should get a minimum of 30 minutes of exercise every day

The largest cat breeds can weigh over 20 pounds

As anyone who has ever been around a cat for any length of time well knows cats have enormous patience with the limitations of the human kind.
-Cleveland Amory
